Super Simple "Socketless" sXMahjong

Mahjong is a four player "draw one, discard one" game in which players attempt to turn a randomly assigned collection of tiles into a winning collection of tiles. Any player may claim another player's discard (even when it would not be the claiming player's turn; claiming makes them the active player) if that tile can be used to form a set of three of a kind, or four of a kind. In addition, suited number tiles may be claimed to form a set of three consecutive same suited numbers, but this claim may not be made out of turn order. A player wins once they have four sets and a pair. While pairs cannot be claimed during normal play, if a tile completes a pair and in so doing forms a winning four sets and a pair, that tile may be claimed as a winning tile (the claim can be made because it's a win, not because it's a pair).
